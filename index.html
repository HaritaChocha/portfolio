About me

At the age of 10 while the big shots in the technology sector were in a race to out-think each other by introducing new ideas with Steve Jobs unveiling the Safari browser, Microsoft upgrading its existing applications, Intel introducing newer upgraded versions of its Celeron processors, I was lost to a world of computer gaming with its vibrant graphics and hidden cheat codes. Afternoons spent staying glued to the computer screen ignited in me a spark of inquisitiveness which has only intensified as technological advancement set on a course to prove Moore’s Law correct.

From computer gaming to computer programming, my fascination towards the computing power to create and revolutionize the world has only gained momentum. I am a Software Developer and Programmer with over 2 years of professional experience as a back-end developer with a zeal to learn new technologies. Currently, I am pursuing my Masters' in Applied Computing at University of Windsor, Windsor, ON and completed my Bachelor's in Computer Engineering from Nirma University, Ahmedabad, India.

Apart from academics and work, I like to spend time learning new technologies and implementing them as projects, teaching subjects like science and maths to children and orphanges, travelling and unveiling adventurous places.

Data Security in Cloud

Journal: International Journal for Engineering Research and Technology (IJERT)

Abstract: It is known well that cloud computing has revolutionized the field of IT enterprise through its potential advantages. Many corporations and industries are moving towards cloud computing era due to its benefits of worry-free hardware maintenance. Still large enterprises are not moving towards the cloud decreasing the amount of market size cloud computing shares. From the consumers perspective, among the various issues involved in moving towards the cloud data security and privacy issues are the major hurdles. This paper describes the various constraints related to data security and privacy protection issues as well as introduces few technologies such SecureCloud™ pioneered by Trend Micro that enables the users to operate safely and securely in cloud. Along with the personal data security on cloud using NoSQL databases.
